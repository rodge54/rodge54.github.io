My Learning Journey

Education was never really my thing growing up. I rarely talked about going to college and when I did go to college I didn't care for it. Once I joined the Air Force I realized education was extremely important to growth as a leader and airman. I started off by taking a couple classes at the local college. I didn't truly get serious about it until I decided I wanted to become a USAF Officer. I completed my undergraduate degree in less than two years. After that I took some time off and decided to pursue a STEM degree.

In 2018, a category 5 hurricane decimated my area. We were sent out of the area and worked only when possible from home. During that time I got bored. I wanted to build a website, but I didn't want to pay for it. As I researched more and more I realized the only way that could happen is if I learned html, css, and javascript. So here I sit, a year and a half later taking my final class for the Masters of Science in Software Development degree at Boston University. P.S. This is my first website.
